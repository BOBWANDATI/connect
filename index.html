Peace Campaigns

About Us

PeaceConnect Ke is a peacebuilding and conflict resolution platform that empowers communities to report conflicts and promote social cohesion.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower communities through peace education, conflict prevention, dialogue and peaceful problem solving using technology and community participation.

Our Vision

Our vision is to create a peaceful, united and violence-free society where people live together with harmony, equality and mutual respect.

Importance of Conflict Prevention

Protects Communities

Conflict prevention helps protect people, families and communities from violence and destruction.

Promotes Unity

Encourages peaceful coexistence, cooperation and understanding among different communities.

Supports Development

Peaceful communities create better opportunities for education, business and social growth.

Encourages Dialogue

Conflict prevention promotes communication, mediation and peaceful problem solving.

Core Values

Peace

Promoting peaceful coexistence within communities.

Unity

Bringing people together through dialogue and understanding.

Justice

Encouraging fairness, equality and respect for human rights.
